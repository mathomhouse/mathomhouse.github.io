Top War Community – We Need Your Voice!

(We can make polls too.)

Eternal Land is expected to return in about 20 days. As you know, we just finished three months of SSC, are completely burnt out, and have not slept since February. This is a pivotal moment for our community to make our voices heard on a topic of genuine, urgent importance.

For a long time, EL participation has been structured like this:

1 server full of people who are tired  +  7 servers full of people who are also tired

This usually leads to one-sided results where everyone logs off after day one, making EL less fun, but at least it ends faster. The Mathom House community believes it is time to ask the hard questions. Not about matchmaking. About existence itself.

Should we even play EL?
(For EL in particular, and continued participation in video games generally.)

We have created a public voting page where players only need to enter their in-game name, server number, and vote. This is not a petition against Top War. It is simply a way to show the devs what we are going through. Whether they listen remains to be seen. They won't.

If enough players participate, our feedback will be harder to ignore. We will send the results directly to the devs and ask for a clear response, especially since they often say they listen to players. (They don't.)
