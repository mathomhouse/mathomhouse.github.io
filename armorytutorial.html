Battle Report Tutorial

Learn how to upload battle reports, analyze account snapshots, and use advanced inventory scanning tools.

1

What This Tool Does

This feature allows players to upload battle reports and automatically generate a detailed snapshot of an account's stats, equipment, and progression.

Some advanced features may require multiple battle reports for more accurate analysis.
1

Locate Your Battle Report

Open the battle report inside the game and prepare the report you would like to upload.

2

Upload the Report

Drag and drop the report file into the uploader or select it manually using the upload button.

Invalid or cropped screenshots may prevent proper parsing.
1

Review Account Stats

Once processed, the system will generate a complete overview of the account including stats, equipped gear, and other important information.

1

Advanced Inventory Collection

The inventory scanner can collect detailed information about stored items, equipment, and materials.
